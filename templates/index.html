Consumer's Complaints Analysis

Consumer Complaints are valuable dataset that can help to improve the business. How to resolve the complaints is an important measurement of the quality of the customer service. The contents of the complaints provide new insight how to upgrade the current products.
In this project, I analyze the Consumer Complaints database by the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB)

Three different analyses are performed.

First Look : Overall complaints statics
Geograpical View : Where do the complaints come from?
Text Analysis : WHAT IS THE PROBLEM!!!
