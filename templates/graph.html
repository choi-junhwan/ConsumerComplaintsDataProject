Respond Rate

The rate of the complaints timely responded from the company. The color range is about 96% to 100% with mean as 97.5%. Darker color represents higher rate.

Although the respond rate is overall high, complaints from North East states (NY and CT) are better responded, while Southern states (MS, LA) are poorly responded

Consumer disputed rate

The rate of the consumer disputed. The color range is about 15% to 25%, with mean of 20.5%. Darker color represents higher rate

Among the big states, consumer dispute rate in California is high. It implies that the complaints from California and Maryland tend to be controversial and may need additional care.
